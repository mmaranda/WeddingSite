About Us

 

We met in drama club in high school but didn't officially start dating until Matt was off to college in Boston and Nikki was in her senior year. Then we both were off on to new adventures in Boston during college: Matt studied Computer Science at Northeastern and Nikki studied International Relations and French nearby at Boston University. Through crazy roommate stories, two semesters abroad, and countless nights out with friends we stayed together.


After college we began our professional careers working our first real jobs and meeting a ton of great new friends. Then Nikki decided to go to law school and began her four year stint as an evening student at New England Law | Boston. Matt was there during the stress of exams, the late nights writing papers, and, in the end, to celebrate her graduation and being sworn-in to the Massachusetts bar in 2014.


Now after 13 years together, we are excited to begin the next big adventure. Allons-y!
