Reception and Cocktail Hour

 

Immediately following the ceremony we ask that you join us in the Lagoda Rooom of the Whaling Museum for the cocktail hour and after that dinner and dancing in the Harbor View Gallery.

Lagoda Room

  • Cocktail Hour
  • Passed Hors D'oeuvres

The Lagoda Rooom, home of the half scale model of the Bark Lagoda, hosts our cocktail hour with passed hors d'oeuvres. Feel free to look around, however, the museum asks that guests refrain from bringing food or drink aboard the Lagoda.

Harbor View Gallery

  • Dinner
  • Dancing

After the cocktail hour we will adjourn to the Harbor View Gallery on the third floor of the museum. There we will have dinner, dancing, and a remarkable view of the New Bedford working harbor.
