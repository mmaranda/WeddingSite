Ceremony

 

Ceremony to be held at the New Bedford Whaling Museum. Guests may arrive by 5pm. Light refreshments will be served and the formal portion of the ceremony beings at 5:30.

Main Entrance to the the New Bedford Whaling Museum

At 5pm the museum closes to the general public. Between 5 and 5:30 our guests will be permitted in. During that time light refreshments will be served. Feel free to explore the atrium and be sure to check out the reticulated whale skeletons suspended from the ceiling.

A Special Request

We request that our guests try to keep photography and other distractions to a minimum during the ceremony.
